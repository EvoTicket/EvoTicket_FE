# AI Chatbot Usage Policy

This Policy explains how EvoTicket uses AI chatbot features to support users, the limits of AI responses and how user data may be processed when interacting with AI-assisted functions.

## 1. Scope

This Policy applies when users interact with EvoTicket AI features, including:

- Event information assistance.
- Platform usage guidance.
- Help Center and FAQ support.
- Ticketing flow explanations.
- Policy lookup assistance.
- File-assisted or RAG-based support where available.

## 2. Purpose of the AI Chatbot

The AI chatbot is designed to help users:

- Find event information more easily.
- Understand ticket purchase and payment steps.
- Locate relevant policy pages.
- Receive general guidance about ticket management, resale or check-in.
- Get faster support before contacting a human support channel.

## 3. Informational Nature of Responses

AI chatbot responses are informational and may be incomplete, outdated or inaccurate. They do not replace:

- Official event information published by the organizer.
- Order or ticket status shown in the user's account.
- Published EvoTicket policy pages.
- Final support decisions from EvoTicket.
- Legal, financial or professional advice.

If there is a conflict between an AI response and an official policy or order record, the official policy or system record prevails.

## 4. No Automatic Legal Commitment

An AI chatbot response is not automatically a binding legal commitment, refund approval, dispute decision, organizer confirmation or guarantee of event access unless it is separately confirmed through an official EvoTicket channel.

## 5. Data Processed by the AI Chatbot

When using the AI chatbot, EvoTicket may process:

- User questions.
- Conversation context.
- Event or ticket information relevant to the query.
- Policy or help content retrieved by the system.
- Uploaded files or text where file-assisted support is used.
- Technical logs for quality, safety and troubleshooting.

## 6. User Responsibilities

Users should:

- Avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive data.
- Avoid sharing passwords, OTPs, private keys or full banking credentials.
- Verify important information through official pages.
- Contact support for payment, refund, check-in or dispute issues that require human review.

## 7. File Upload and RAG Mode

If EvoTicket provides file upload or RAG-assisted support, the uploaded content may be processed to generate responses for the relevant session or support purpose.

Users must not upload files that they are not authorized to share or files containing unnecessary personal data of other persons.

## 8. Human Escalation

EvoTicket may provide human support escalation for cases involving:

- Payment inconsistency.
- Ticket not issued after payment.
- Refund requests.
- Check-in disputes.
- Resale transfer errors.
- Organizer-related complaints.
- Personal data requests.

## 9. AI Safety and Abuse

EvoTicket may restrict or suspend chatbot access if a user attempts to abuse the system, extract confidential information, generate harmful content, submit unlawful content or interfere with platform security.

## 10. Legal References

This Policy may be interpreted together with:

- Law No. 19/2023/QH15 on Protection of Consumers' Rights.
- Decree No. 13/2023/ND-CP on Personal Data Protection.
- Law No. 91/2025/QH15 on Personal Data Protection.
- Law No. 20/2023/QH15 on Electronic Transactions.
- Relevant Vietnamese policies on digital transformation and artificial intelligence where applicable.
