# Privacy and Personal Data Processing Policy

This Policy explains how EvoTicket collects, uses, stores, protects, shares and otherwise processes personal data when users access the platform, create accounts, buy tickets, list or buy resale tickets, register as organizers, check in at events or use AI-assisted features.

## 1. Scope

This Policy applies to:

- Buyers and ticket holders.
- Organizers and their representatives.
- Checkers and event staff using platform tools.
- Users who contact support or use the AI chatbot.
- Visitors of the EvoTicket website or application.

## 2. Categories of Data We May Process

Depending on the feature used, EvoTicket may process the following data categories.

### 2.1. Account Data

- Full name.
- Email address.
- Phone number.
- Login credentials or authentication records.
- Account role, status and preferences.
- Login history, session data and security logs.

### 2.2. Buyer and Ticketing Data

- Event, showtime, ticket type and seat selection.
- Order code and order status.
- Ticket code, ticket status and access status.
- Ticket ownership and transfer history.
- QR version, check-in status and check-in time.
- Support requests and complaint records.

### 2.3. Payment Data

EvoTicket may process payment-related records such as payment method, transaction reference, payment status, payment time, payment gateway response, reconciliation information and refund handling status.

EvoTicket does not need to store full payment card details if the payment is handled by a third-party payment provider.

### 2.4. Organizer Data

For organizer registration and operation, EvoTicket may process:

- Organization name and business information.
- Representative or contact person information.
- Business email and phone number.
- Verification documents.
- Tax, bank account or payout-related information.
- Event, ticket inventory, venue and settlement records.

### 2.5. Dynamic QR and Check-In Data

EvoTicket may process QR token claims, ticket access state, event ID, showtime ID, ticket asset ID, owner ID, QR version, device/checker information, scan time, scan result and check-in logs.

### 2.6. Resale Data

Where resale is enabled, EvoTicket may process listing price, price cap, seller ID, buyer ID, listing status, reservation window, platform fee, payout status, ownership transfer status and dispute information.

### 2.7. Blockchain and NFT Data

Where blockchain/NFT features are enabled, EvoTicket may process technical data such as wallet address, token ID, transaction hash, smart contract address, mint status, transfer status and provenance records.

EvoTicket aims to avoid recording directly identifiable personal data on public blockchain infrastructure where reasonably possible.

### 2.8. AI Chatbot and RAG Data

When a user interacts with the AI chatbot, EvoTicket may process:

- Questions and prompts.
- Conversation context.
- Support intent and retrieved knowledge snippets.
- Uploaded files or text where file-assisted support is used.
- Technical logs needed to improve safety, quality and troubleshooting.

### 2.9. Technical Data

EvoTicket may process IP address, user agent, device information, cookies or similar identifiers, access logs, error logs and security events.

## 3. Purposes of Processing

EvoTicket processes data for the following purposes:

- Account creation, authentication and role management.
- Ticket purchase, order handling and payment confirmation.
- Digital ticket issuance and ticket lifecycle management.
- Dynamic QR generation and check-in verification.
- Official resale listing, reservation and ownership transfer.
- Organizer verification, event management and settlement support.
- Customer support, complaint handling and dispute resolution.
- Fraud prevention, security monitoring and system integrity.
- AI-assisted user support and event information retrieval.
- Compliance with applicable legal obligations.

## 4. Legal Basis and Consent

Depending on the specific processing activity, EvoTicket may process personal data based on:

- The data subject's consent.
- The need to perform a transaction or provide a requested service.
- Legal obligations applicable to the platform, organizers or payment flow.
- Legitimate rights and interests where permitted by law, such as fraud prevention, dispute handling and system security.

Consent must be expressed in a form that can be recorded, verified, copied or printed where required. EvoTicket does not treat silence as consent where explicit consent is required.

## 5. Sharing of Data

EvoTicket may share necessary data with:

- Organizers for event operation, attendee verification, support and dispute handling.
- Payment service providers for payment processing, confirmation and reconciliation.
- Email, notification, cloud, hosting, security or analytics providers.
- Blockchain infrastructure or web3 service providers where NFT/provenance functions are enabled.
- AI service providers where AI chatbot or RAG features require technical processing.
- Competent authorities where a valid legal request applies.

EvoTicket does not sell users' personal data.

## 6. Cross-Border Processing

Some technical providers may process or store data outside Vietnam. Where cross-border transfer rules apply, EvoTicket will follow applicable requirements on transfer assessment, recordkeeping and compliance documentation.

## 7. Blockchain Data Notice

Public blockchain records may be persistent and technically difficult or impossible to remove in the same way as off-chain database records. EvoTicket therefore aims to minimize personal data on-chain and use technical references, hashes or identifiers rather than directly identifiable personal information where reasonably possible.

Requests to delete or correct data may be fulfilled through off-chain records, account data and platform displays where direct blockchain alteration is not technically possible.

## 8. AI Chatbot Data Notice

AI chatbot interactions may be stored for support continuity, safety review, troubleshooting and service quality. Users should not submit unnecessary sensitive data, financial credentials, identity documents or private information through the chatbot unless the platform specifically requests it for a support process.

AI responses are informational and do not replace official policy, event information or human support decisions.

## 9. Data Security

EvoTicket applies reasonable organizational and technical safeguards, including role-based access, logging, system monitoring, access control, secure configuration and incident response processes.

No online platform can guarantee absolute security. Users should protect their own accounts, devices and authentication methods.

## 10. Data Retention

EvoTicket retains personal data for as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this Policy, including ticket lifecycle management, payment reconciliation, legal compliance, dispute handling, fraud prevention and audit requirements.

Data may be retained for longer where required by law, pending disputes, unresolved payment issues or security investigations.

## 11. User Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may have rights to:

- Be informed about data processing.
- Give or withdraw consent.
- Access personal data.
- Correct inaccurate data.
- Request deletion or restriction of processing.
- Object to certain processing activities.
- Request data provision where applicable.
- Complain, report violations or seek compensation under applicable law.

Requests should be submitted through the official support or privacy contact channel displayed by EvoTicket.

## 12. Children's Data

If a user is under the age at which independent consent is legally recognized, a parent or legal guardian may be required to provide consent or supervise platform use, depending on applicable law and event rules.

## 13. Cookies and Local Storage

EvoTicket may use cookies, local storage or similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, improve security, analyze performance and support platform functions.

Users may manage browser settings, but disabling certain technologies may affect login, checkout, ticket display or security features.

## 14. Personal Data Incidents

If a personal data incident occurs, EvoTicket will assess the scope, take mitigation measures and notify users or competent authorities where required by applicable law.

## 16. Updates

EvoTicket may update this Policy from time to time. The version and effective date are shown at the top of this document. Material changes may require renewed consent where applicable.
